We are Executive Creative Directors at BBDO New York, currently overseeing Snickers and Mars petcare brands. We created the “You’re not you when you’re hungry” campaign for Snickers. It began with the 2010 admeter-winning commercial starting Betty White, and has since gone on to win every major award from Cannes Lions to Effies, while helping Snickers grow from the #7 to #1 global chocolate bar. Most recently, we won the One Show Penta award, honoring long term client-agency relationships. And we picked up or second Super Clio for 2020's “Fix the World” Super Bowl campaign.


We also work on US and global advertising for Mars petcare brands, including Pedigree’s Dogs on Zoom campaign to facilitate dog adoption during quarantine. And during our tenure at BBDO, we have written and overseen award winning work for FedEx, Mountain Dew, Twix, and others.
